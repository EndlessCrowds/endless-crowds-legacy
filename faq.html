Frequently Asked Questions

What is Endless Crowds?

Endless Crowds is a crowdfunding platform dedicated to supporting America's military members, first responders, and their families.

How much does it cost?

Starting a project is free. We deduct a small 5% service fee from donations to help us find corporate partners and raise awareness.

Who can raise money?

Anyone can raise money for causes related to our heroes. This includes charities, individuals, and first responder departments.

Need Help?

Visit our Help Center for more information.
