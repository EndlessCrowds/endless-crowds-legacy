Our Vision

To serve people of the Detroit metropolitan area by making philanthropic grants and providing services that contribute to the health of the community.

Grants

Funded by proceeds from EndlessCrowds.com & generous donors the foundation makes monetary distributions to 501(c)(3) organizations, established charitable programs, and to individuals who reside within the Detroit metropolitan area.

Specifically, the foundation provides funding dedicated to
  • Education
  • Arts & recreation
  • Small business

Our support is primarily aimed at existing charitable programs and organizations, but may also be distributed for the creation and development of new ventures with objectives that are consistent with the Foundation's purposes.
